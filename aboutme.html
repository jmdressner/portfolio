About Me

Welcome to my website!


I am someone who loves to travel, to be out in nature, and try new foods with friends. But I am just as happy to spend the day losing myself in a good book or working on a piece of art. I love to try and learn new things and understand more about the amazing world around me. Sometimes accused of being a perfectionist, I work hard and try to do my best at everything to do.
