Home

Home is a fluid term for me. I have lived in 14 houses, 9 dorm rooms, in 4 countries over the past 25 years. As much as I enjoy traveling and experiencing new cultures it makes it difficult to answer the question of Where are you from?

Milwaukee

Milwaukee is where I am living now. This is where my mother grew up and where my father spent part of his childhood. It is where we always came to visit when I was a child and when we moved back to American is it where my parents chose to move to.

Israel/Palestine

After graduating from university the opportunity came up for me to go to Bethlehem and work at a small American school. I jumped at the chance to go back overseas and loved it so much I stayed a second year.

Stevens Point

This is where I first lived as an adult in America. I attended the University of Wisconsin Stevens Point and graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Dietetics.

India

During my highschool years I attended a small international boarding school in South India. Though there were many hard things about living in a boarding school, I loved those years and cherish the friendships that I made there.

Bangladesh

This is where my parents worked for 22 years. I was born in the USA when my parents were there visiting family, but moved back to Bangladesh after a few months. I spent the majority of my childhood there living in Dhaka, the capital city.
